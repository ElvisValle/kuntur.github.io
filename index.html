General Description

This journey is an invitation to awaken. Walk through ancient temples and experience an ancestral ceremony with Wachuma (San Pedro) guided by an Andean master in the Sacred Valley — a safe and natural environment for introspection, energy cleansing, and deep spiritual connection. Then, travel to Machu Picchu, the energetic heart of the Andean world, to complete your transformation with clarity and strength.
Ideal for those who seek more than tourism: a true awakening.

Cusco City Tour: Catedral, Koricancha, Sacsayhuamán, Qenqo, Puca Pucara y Tambomachay.

Andean Wachuma Ceremony (Sacred Valley): Transfer to the valley, welcome by the Andean master at the retreat house, ceremony start, nature walk, meditation, group reflection, light meal, and integration.

Machu Picchu Full Day: Tourist train from Ollantaytambo, bus to the Sanctuary, guided tour, and return.
